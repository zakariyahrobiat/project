MILK
is an important source of food for human, either fresh
fluid or processed into a number of diary products such
as butter and cheese. milk is essensially an emulsion of
fat, protein, sugar, minerals, and vitamins.
Drinking milk may prevent osteoporosis and bone
fractures and even help to maintain healthy health and
weight.

  • vitamin B12:18% of the RDA
  • potassium:10% of the RDA
  • phosphorus:22% of the RDA
